Last updated: 2026-06-29

These Terms apply to the public alpha version of omyscan by Dr.PyLab.

## What omyscan is

omyscan is a passive agent-facing surface mapper. It helps identify what AI agents may be able to read, discover or attempt to use on public web targets, including signals such as llms.txt, AI bot policies, MCP manifests, OpenAPI/OAuth metadata and action surfaces.

omyscan findings are exposure signals intended for review. They are not vulnerability claims.

## Public alpha

omyscan is currently provided as an alpha product. Features, outputs, limits, pricing and availability may change.

## Allowed use

You may use omyscan to scan public web targets where you have a legitimate reason to review agent-facing exposure.

You must not use omyscan to:

- scan private, internal or unauthorized systems;
- bypass access controls;
- fuzz, exploit, attack or disrupt services;
- submit secrets, passwords, tokens or confidential URLs;
- use the service for abuse, spam, harassment or illegal activity.

## Passive scanning only

Hosted omyscan is designed to use passive GET/HEAD requests against limited, allowlisted paths. It does not intentionally exploit, brute-force, bypass authentication or perform active penetration testing.
